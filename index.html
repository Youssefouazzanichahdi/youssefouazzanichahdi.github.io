Bio

I am a PhD student at MICS, CentraleSupélec, under the joint supervision of Damien Challet and Mathieu Rosenbaum.

From 2022 to 2023, I completed the Master 2 in Probability and Finance (ex-DEA El Karoui) at École Polytechnique & Sorbonne Université, Paris. From 2019 to 2022, I studied at Grenoble INP – ENSIMAG, where I obtained a Master of Engineering, alongside a Master 2 in Quantitative Finance from IAE Grenoble.

I have held positions at GTS (Global Trading Systems) as a Quantitative Researcher, at Mazars as a Quantitative Analyst, and at CDG Capital in Risk Management.

Research interests

  • Market microstructure — price formation & discovery, limit order books, agent based models.
  • Market impact — passive & agressive impact.
  • Optimal execution & trading — stochastic control, optimisation.
  • Volatility — Volatility estimation and rough volatility.
  • Financial econometrics — High-frequency data, statistical inference.
